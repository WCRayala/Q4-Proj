Melanie Martinez

Melanie Adele Martinez, an American singer-songwriter, screenwriter, poet, director, actress, dancer, producer, and photographer.

She was born on April 28, 1995, in Queens, Astoria, grew up in Baldwin, New York. She claimed to have "loved singing since she was really little" and that, at the age of 14, she taught herself how to play the guitar by searching for and studying the chord diagrams of songs she enjoyed, which allowed her to write her own songs as well. She says that her passion for poetry inspired her to write songs.

After making an appearance on season three of the American television talent show The Voice, Martinez became well-known in 2012.After making an appearance on season three of the American television talent show The Voice, Martinez became well-known in 2012.

Despite getting eliminated in the Top 6 in the voice, Melanie started working on her own original music. On February 9th, 2014, Melanie released her single "Dollhouse" and a music video for it.
